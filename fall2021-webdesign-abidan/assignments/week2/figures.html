Figures

The figure element is usually used for "attaching" text to an image. Note the difference between the alt text and the figcaption text, they usually have different purposes.

Quotes and Blockquotes

The blockquote is to quote text as BLOCK-LEVEL element, usually for quoting large amounts of text, or text that is not quoted within your own paragraphs/text.
